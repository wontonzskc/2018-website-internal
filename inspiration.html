Inspirations

I used these photos to base my model off. The turbo in my hammer was inspired by the engine of a plane, I thought I could make the turbo of my hammer look more interesting by changing the angle of the blades to be a lot steeper, and I had added more layers of blades. I put rings of light around the handle of the hammer, and I gave the connector between the main body of the hammer and the handle a pattern. I gave the hammer a gunmetal color, the connector a nickel color. I gave the entire hammer (excluding the rings on the handle and the entire turbo) a scratched texture to make it look some what used. I think that my model has color, line (since it has the textures and patterns), size (from the different sized cylinders), texture and value (from the rendered images)
